Use Case: Sacred Spaces (Rural Parish/Community Hub)
The Stewardship Model

For small, high-trust communities, EbD provides "Enterprise-Grade" protection without the complexity. It is about preserving the "Heart" of the community.

The Problem: Lack of IT staff, vulnerability to phishing/scams, and the "Abandonment" of volunteers by complex tools.

The EbD Solution:

Plug-and-Play Sovereignty: The Parish uses the Aether Veil hardware to manage the "Church Roll" and donations. It ensures that the names and needs of the congregation never leave the building.

Keating as Parish Steward: Keating helps the Pastor and volunteer secretary manage outreach and scheduling, providing "Gentle Guidance" that respects their time and tech-fluency.

The Sanctuary Safety Net: A simplified "Zero-Harm" protocol that uses ambient sensors to ensure the building is secure and safe for elderly parishioners during off-hours.

Graduation Metric: 100% volunteer retention and total administrative peace of mind through "Privacy-at-Intake."
