# **Phase 1: Institutional Triage (The "Analog-to-Digital" Bridge)**

This document outlines the first 180 days of modernization for an educational institution currently operating on 20th-century analog infrastructure. We are moving the school from "Paper-Persistence" to the **Holokleric Standard**.

## **1\. The Audit (The "Mimeograph Hunt")**

Before we install a single NPU, we must map the "Chaos."

* **Workflow Forensic Audit:** Identify every process that currently requires physical paper, rubber cement, or manual file-cabinet retrieval (e.g., enrollment, transcripts, teacher lesson plans).  
* **The "Friction Map":** Document the time-tax on staff. If a teacher spends 4 hours a week on manual "Letterhead" production, that is a 10% loss of educational capacity.  
* **Legacy Asset Preservation:** Identify which historical records must be digitized immediately to prevent data loss (rot, fire, or misplacement).

## **2\. Setting Up the Digital Bedrock**

We bypass the "1998 PC" problem by introducing the **EbD Hardware Sieve**.

* **The "Sieve-Stick" Rollout:** Every administrative desk receives an **Aether Veil** hardware unit. This creates an instant "Secure by Default" environment without needing to replace every aging desktop immediately.  
* **The Sovereign Server:** Establish a local, NPU-driven data vault. All student records are moved from cabinets to the **Bauta Valve**, where they are sieved and symbolized for modern retrieval.

## **3\. Keating’s Role: The Bridge from 1998**

For a staff that hasn't seen an update in nearly 30 years, technology is a threat. Keating’s job is to mitigate that fear.

* **The "No-Teacher-Left-Behind" Initiative:** Keating provides one-on-one sessions focused on "High-Fidelity Human Centricity." He doesn't teach "Software"; he teaches **Sovereignty**.  
* **The Commons (The Virtual Teachers' Lounge):** We launch a digital "Commons" where staff can coordinate. Keating presides over this space, helping them move their manual "Letterhead" templates into clean, accessible-by-default digital formats.  
* **Removing the Accessibility Tax:** Keating identifies staff members who have been "working around" old technology due to sensory or cognitive friction and provides immediate EbD-standard accommodations.

## **4\. Operational Deliverables (Month 6\)**

* **Zero-Paper Enrollment:** The first fully digital, sieved enrollment cycle is completed. No mimeographs, no manual filing.  
* **The "Sovereign Roll":** A unified, de-identified database of the student body that allows administration to see attendance and progress in real-time without compromising student privacy.  
* **Staff Stabilization:** 90% of personnel are "Keating-Certified" in the basic digital workflows, reporting a measurable decrease in "Administrative Burnout."

---

### **🏛️ The "Holokleric" Result**

By the end of Phase 1, the institution has "Graduated" from 1998\. They are now a **Modern Production Base**. They have a clean foundation, a secure privacy valve, and a staff that feels mentored rather than abandoned.

Now, and only now, can we begin to discuss the "Classroom" use case and advanced learning tools.
