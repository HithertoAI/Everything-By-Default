Use Case: Community Education
The Sovereign Classroom

In education, the "By Default" principle shifts from safety to Agency. We protect the student’s future by ensuring their data is never a commodity.

The Problem: Predatory "Ed-Tech" tracking and teachers overwhelmed by administrative "Legacy Chaos."

The EbD Solution:

The Sovereign Student Profile: Students use a localized "Aether Veil" stick. Their learning progress, neurodivergent needs, and behavioral data stay in their own "Chamber A," shared only as de-identified "Intent" with the school.

Keating as Co-Tutor: Keating doesn't just train the staff; he acts as a personalized learning mentor for students, adapting the curriculum to their sensory and cognitive profiles.

The Commons (The Study Hall): A secure digital space where students collaborate on projects without being tracked by third-party social or search algorithms.

Graduation Metric: 100% student data sovereignty and a verified "Zero Lost Potential" benchmark for neurodivergent learners.
