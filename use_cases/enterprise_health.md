Use Case: Enterprise Health (1,000-Bed System)
The Holokleric Model for Clinical Excellence

At this scale, modernization is a matter of life and death. The EbD framework is deployed to eliminate the "Accessibility Tax" on clinicians and the "Death by Data" risk for patients.

The Problem: Fragmented EHRs, vendor bloat, and a 400% increase in clinician burnout.

The EbD Solution:

The Bauta Valve High-Velocity Gate: Real-time telemetry from surgical suites and ICU beds is sieved through local NPUs before hitting the central data lake.

Keating’s Clinical Integration: Keating provides "Just-in-Time" training for new surgical robots or pharmaceutical dispensers, ensuring that surgeons and nurses are never "guessing" at a new interface.

The Vendor Gate: All third-party portfolio companies (Imaging, Labs, Billing) must pass the EbD Rubric to prevent "Science Experiments" from compromising the patient safety net.

Graduation Metric: A certified 90% reduction in preventable medical errors and a 15% improvement in operational margin through the licensing of Anonymized Clinical Corpora.
