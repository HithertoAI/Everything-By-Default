# **Phase 2: Core Modernization & Portfolio Integration (Months 7–18)**

The objective of Phase 2 is to build the unified transactional platform and begin controlled pilots of vetted technologies on a stable, secure foundation.

## **1\. Key Objectives & OKRs**

* O1: Establish a modern, unified data platform.  
  * **KR1:** Achieve 95%+ uptime for the new standardized core (e.g., EHR, ERP, or PACS).  
  * **KR2:** Migrate 80% of analytical data to the secure-by-default cloud platform.  
* O2: Validate the Portfolio Integration Model.  
  * **KR1:** Demonstrate a measurable reduction in a specific "Preventable Harm" metric (e.g., a 40% reduction in medication errors or data breaches) across 3–5 successfully piloted solutions.

## **2\. Portfolio Integration Protocol (The "Vendor Gate")**

In Large Enterprise and Government scenarios, Hitherto AI manages the client’s existing portfolio companies and set vendor lists.

* **The Innovation Review Board (IRB):** All client-preferred technologies are vetted against a mandatory EbD rubric.  
* **Rubric Vetting:** Technologies must score for inherent security, interoperability, and scalability.  
* **The Gate:** No technology is introduced to the live environment without passing this gate, ensuring that the "By Default" principle is maintained even with third-party tools.

## **3\. Technology Workstreams: Building the Bedrock**

* **Standardization:** Roll out a single, optimized instance of the core transactional layer (e.g., EHR or central database).  
* **The Data Fabric:** Deploy a medical device or IoT integration platform to unify real-time data into the central ontology.  
* **Sustainable Backbone:** Replace legacy systems with cloud-based, vendor-neutral archives to create a sustainable imaging or data backbone.

## **4\. Human Capital: Advanced Upskilling**

* **Clinical/Operational Integration Units:** Dedicated, co-located teams of staff and IT personnel redesign workflows specifically for the new platform to ensure efficiency by default.  
* **Keating’s Just-in-Time Training:** Keating provides hyper-specific training modules at the point of care/action for all newly integrated portfolio technologies.  
* **Reskilling Programs:** Identify roles at risk of automation and begin training for new, high-value roles, demonstrating a "Sustainable by Default" approach to workforce evolution.

## **5\. Governance & Continuity**

* **Compliance Hardening:** Formalize the process for classifying portfolio technologies under relevant regulatory bodies (e.g., FDA SaMD), ensuring efficacy by default.  
* **Parallel Operations:** Run legacy and new systems in parallel for critical units to ensure a "Secure by Default" transition with zero interruption to safety.  
* **The "Code Red" Protocol:** Establish a clear chain of command for any critical safety issues, preserving the integrity of the platform and its data.

## **6\. Data Asset Creation: "Clinical/Operational Workflow Integration"**

This phase captures how staff interact with and adapt to new technologies in real-time.

* **Human Feedback Data:** This captures rejection points and efficiency gains, which is essential for moving a tool from a "science project" to a viable product.  
* **AI Fluency Dataset:** Data from training and upskilling forms a uniquely valuable asset for research into human-AI collaboration.
