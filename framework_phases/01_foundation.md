# **Phase 1: Foundation & Triage (Months 0–6)**

The primary objective of Phase 1 is to stabilize the existing environment and build foundational trust with all stakeholders through transparent, secure processes.

## **1\. Key Objectives & OKRs**

* **O1: Establish a secure and stable operational baseline.**  
  * **KR1:** Achieve 70% operational stability within the new zero-trust security framework.  
  * **KR2:** Complete a full technology and process audit mapped against the "By Default" principle.  
* **O2: Build foundational trust through the "Mentorship Layer."**  
  * **KR1:** Launch and achieve 30%+ adoption of **The Commons**.  
  * **KR2:** Complete a "listening tour" with 100+ key leaders to co-design the human integration layer.

## **2\. The Implementation of "The Commons"**

We instantiate the secure and accessible-by-default environment by standing up a unified communication and mentorship hub.

* **Keating Integration:** Keating acts as an always-available mentor within **The Commons**, providing just-in-time training and support for all staff.  
* **The Executive Mandate:** Participation in **The Commons** is mandatory for the C-Suite. This ensures leadership understands the "Organizational Physiology"—the real-time map of adoption patterns and friction points—rather than relying on filtered reports.  
* **Sandboxing:** **The Commons** provides a zero-risk space to validate "Principle in Action" pilots, such as AI scheduling or digital portals.

## **3\. Technology Workstreams**

Modernization begins with the structural "Nervous System" of the organization.

* **Secure by Default Foundation:** Deployment of campus-wide SD-WAN and a zero-trust architecture.  
* **Efficient Core:** Establishment of a secure, HIPAA-compliant cloud tenant with a central data lake and a unified data ontology.  
* **Standardization Gap Analysis:** A rigorous audit of all legacy systems (EHR, PACS, etc.) to create a single, optimized template for Phase 2\.

## **4\. Human Capital & Governance**

We ensure the transition is sustainable by preparing the workforce and the legal framework.

* **Foundational Fluency:** Launch core modules focused on tool literacy and the legal/ethical landscape of AI.  
* **The Living Lab Charter:** Establish a clear, public-facing framework for data anonymization and patient/user consent. This transforms potential data liabilities into a trusted brand asset.  
* **Innovation Review Board (IRB):** Form the board and ratify the "By Default" integration rubric to vet all incoming technologies.

## **5\. Data Asset Creation: "Organizational Physiology"**

The interactions within **The Commons** and the initial technology audit create the baseline "Organizational Physiology" dataset.

* **ROI Verification:** This maps digital fluency and friction points before transformation, providing the critical control group needed to measure true ROI in later phases.  
* **Executive Visibility:** Executives who engage with Keating gain direct insight into this "gold" dataset, preventing the "drift" that leads to failed implementations.

---

### **🏁 Gate 1: The Month 6 Decision**

Approval to proceed to Phase 2 is dependent on verified adherence to the "By Default" principle in pilots, stable operations, and positive feedback from the staff and leadership "listening tours".
