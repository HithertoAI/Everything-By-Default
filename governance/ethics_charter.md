# **The EbD Ethics Charter: Data Sovereignty & The Clean Guarantee**

We do not "govern" data after it is collected; we engineer its integrity at the point of origin.

* **The Clean Guarantee:** All data generated within an EbD environment is sieved through the **Bauta Valve**. We guarantee that licensed datasets are 100% PII-free and ethically cleared.  
* **Non-Exploitation:** We reject the "Surveillance Capitalism" model. The user is the sovereign owner of their intent.  
* **Stewardship over Ownership:** The organization does not "own" the human; it stewards the environment in which the human thrives.  
* **The Zero-Harm Mandate:** Technology must never be a bystander to preventable harm. If a "Harm Pattern" is detected, the **Overseer Chamber (Ω)** and **Keating** are authorized to intervene.
